Privacy Policy for Frog Loop

Effective date: June 11, 2026 · Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Artem Svirinovskyi (“we”, “us”) handles information in the mobile game Frog Loop (the “Game”), available on the Apple App Store and Google Play. By using the Game you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

The Game is playable without creating an account. Some data is collected automatically to run core features (leaderboard, ads, analytics), and some only if you choose to sign in.

DataWhenWhy
Anonymous user ID Automatically at first launch Identifies your device for the leaderboard and saved progress, without any personal details.
Email address & name Only if you sign in with Apple or Google To create and manage your account and sync progress across devices. (Apple “Hide My Email” is supported.)
Nickname, country, score and game progress During play / when you edit your profile To show the global and per-country leaderboard and to save your progress in the cloud.
Advertising ID & approximate location (from IP) While ads are shown Used by Google AdMob to serve ads. See “Advertising” below.
Gameplay events, app interactions, anonymous install ID, device & coarse-location info During play Anonymous analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase) to understand and improve the Game.
Purchase history If you buy “Remove Ads” Processed via RevenueCat to deliver and restore your purchase across devices.

Stored only on your device: your profile photo / avatar never leaves your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

2. How we use information

  • Run core game features: leaderboards, cloud save and cross-device progress.
  • Create and manage your optional account.
  • Show advertising and offer an optional ad-free purchase.
  • Measure and improve gameplay through anonymous analytics.
  • Prevent cheating and abuse, and keep the service secure.

3. Advertising

The Game shows ads through Google AdMob. In the European Economic Area, the UK and other regions where required, you will be shown a consent form (Google’s User Messaging Platform) where you can choose between personalized and non-personalized ads. On iOS, the Game also asks for App Tracking Transparency permission. AdMob may use your advertising identifier and approximate location to serve ads. Learn more: How Google uses advertising data.

4. Third-party services

We share data only with the service providers needed to run the Game:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, cloud database, analytics) — Privacy & Security
  • Google AdMob (advertising) — Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchase management) — RevenueCat Privacy Policy

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Data retention and deletion

We keep account and progress data while your account exists. You can permanently delete your account and all associated cloud data directly in the Game: open Settings → your account → Delete account. This removes your leaderboard entry, your cloud save and your purchase identity from our systems.

You may also request deletion by contacting us at support@vias.im and we will delete your data.

6. Security

All data transmitted between the Game and our service providers is encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS.

7. Children’s privacy

The Game is intended for users aged 13 and older. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

8. International users

Our service providers may process and store data on servers located outside your country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location (for example under GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data, or object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, use the in-game account deletion or contact us at the email below.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date above and, for significant changes, provide notice within the Game or store listing.
